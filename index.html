Momo

Momo is a type of dumpling. It is popular throughout most of the Indian subcontinent and in the regions straddling the Himalayas between South Asia and East

Chowmin

Chowmin is a seasoned stew of shredded or diced meat, mushrooms, and vegetables that is usually served with fried noodles

Samosa

A samosa is a South Asian fried or baked pastry with a savory filling like spiced potatoes, onions, peas, chicken and other meats, or lentils

Yamari

Yomari, also called Yamari, is a delicacy of the Newar community in Nepal. It is a steamed dumpling that consists of an external covering of rice flour with sweet fillings such as chaku.
